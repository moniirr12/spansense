Dashboard

Everything at a glance

The home screen surfaces live KPIs, condition charts, and critical structure alerts the moment you log in.

  • Total assets, high-risk count, upcoming & overdue inspections
  • Asset inventory by type — pie chart
  • BCI score distribution across the portfolio
  • Bridge condition trend over time
Interactive Map

Your structure stock on the map

Every structure plotted with colour-coded markers by type. Click any marker to open its full record, view BCI scores, and start a new inspection.

Inspection

Element-by-element defect recording

Log inspections span by span. Record severity, extent, and works required per element — BCI scores calculate in real time.

Inspection History

Full history per structure

Every inspection is stored and filterable by type. BCI trends are tracked automatically over time.

  • Filter by Principal (PI), General (GI), or Superficial (SI)
  • CRIT and AV scores shown per row
  • Total inspection count and last inspection date
  • Open, edit, or generate a report from any past record
