Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export inspection data?
Head to the Database page, select your bridge records, choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, or XML), and click "Export Selected". You can also filter by structure type before exporting.
Can I integrate spanSense with my existing asset management system?
Yes — we offer a REST API and webhook support for enterprise customers. Reach out via the Partnership option above and our integrations team will get you set up.
What happens to the data if I cancel my subscription?
Your data remains accessible in read-only mode for 90 days after cancellation. You can export everything before that grace period ends. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted per our retention policy.
How is the inspection data secured?
All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3. We are ISO 27001 certified and conduct annual penetration testing. Backups are geo-redundant and retained for 7 years per industry standards.
