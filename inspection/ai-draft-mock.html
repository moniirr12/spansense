AI Draft Assistant

Right now, inspectors open the "Add Defect" modal once per finding and manually set the element, severity, extent, works, priority, cost and comment. This concept flips the order: capture photos and notes quickly in the field, then let AI propose a complete draft of defect entries for the inspector to review, correct and confirm. Nothing below is saved or sent anywhere — it's a clickable mock of the workflow.
